"Work on what is important, not just what is interesting. There is an infinite supply of both"

I like oligonucleotides and and want to use them to revolutionize medicine. Like our own DNA and RNA they can be represented as strings of letters (ATCCGCCTGCCG). This makes them very amenable to bioinformatic sequence analysis, which I like to call oligoinformatics.
